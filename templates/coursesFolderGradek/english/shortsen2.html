Sentence Framing- Part 2

Kids should follow below points:(Parents read this instructions and teach your child)

Practice consistently

Engage your child in meaningful writing from day one. Kid learn to write by writing even if it is a large string of letters at first or even scribbling. so do practice

Use our drawing board

Use our drawing board to write words and sentences and parents should encourgae invented spelling.

Try interactive writing

Kids should write down to the white board present in our site to help sound out words and place appropriate punctuation of morning messages or night messages, etc

Choose meaningful topics

Let them write about the topics they choose in their journals.Sentence starters confuse kindergarten students. If they write about the same thing for a while, it is ok. It is much like reading the same book over and over again. They are building confidence
