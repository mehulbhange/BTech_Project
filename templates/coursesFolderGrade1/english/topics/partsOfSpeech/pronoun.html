What is Pronoun?

Pronouns are the words that are used in place of nouns in sentences. Such as I, me, you, your, he, him, his, she, her, it, we, us, our, you, your, they, them, their etc. Pronouns are used to enrich the reading and writing experience by eliminating repetitive use of noun.

Examples of Pronoun used in Sentences

He likes to eat pizza.

She went to park.

Mr. Lee bought a house after he had saved enough money.

Mr. and Mrs. Kelly may not attend the party as they are on vacation right now.
