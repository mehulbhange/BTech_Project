Subtract a one-digit number to a two-digit number

Kid should watch this lesson carefully and parents should teach below points to their child

This topic will help student to get better understanding of subtraction of two digit number with one digit. This topic will build a strong base for future topics.

Apart from the examples taken in the video lecture parents should give some other examples in order to train your kids.
