Count up to 100

Once children can count reliably their knowledge of the number sequence can be extended to count both forwards and backwards, from any given number.

In this module we are going to learns numbers upto 100 so that students are able to do day to day evaluations

  • Counting - up to 100
  • Counting tens and ones - up to 99
  • Counting by twos, fives and tens with pictures
  • Counting forward and backward

Addition and subtraction

Addition helps kids master the relationships between numbers and understand how quantities relate to one another. Even when kindergartners can't reliably answer addition problems or manipulate large numbers, basic addition skills give them a framework for mastering math in elementary school.

Kid will learn following Topics:

  • Addition sentences using number lines
  • Addition word problems
  • Addition sentences for word problems
  • Make a number using addition
  • Add a one-digit number to a two-digit number
  • Add a two-digit number to a two-digit number

Subtraction is apart of our everyday lives and therefore an important concept to develop early. We need to understand how to subtract in order to engage with society effectively as we use subtraction when dealing with money, cooking, travel and time, among countless other daily experiences.

Kid will learn following Topics:

  • Complete the subtraction sentence
  • Subtraction word problems
  • Subtraction sentences using number lines
  • Make a number using subtraction
  • Subtract a one-digit number from a two-digit number
  • Subtract a one-digit number from a two-digit number

Multiplication and Division

Multiplication is a main tool for many forms of maths. The ability to rehearse and understand multiplications up to and including 12 by the final year of primary school will enable your child to confidently and skilfully tackle the more complex mathematical subjects.

Small multiplication of numbers will be present in this topic to give brief idea about multiplication.

The division is a method of distributing a group of things into equal parts. It is one of the four basic operations of arithmetic, which gives a fair result of sharing.

Small division of numbers will be present in this topic to give brief idea about multiplication.
