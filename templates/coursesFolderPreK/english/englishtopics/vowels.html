Learn-Introduction to Vowels

Parent need to teach following points in order to learn and sound vowels.

Listen for the vowel:

Say one word at a time. Students will do the hand motion for the vowel they hear. This activity will attach a movement essential for kinesthetic learners and will also allow you to do a quick visual check to see if every child correctly identified the vowel.Sample word list :Sand, tent, with, bus, off, Tom, bat, best, fresh, sun, punt.

Play Quick Draw:

Each child has a white board, or if you are as lucky as I was, you will have enough room on your big white board to accommodate all the kids in your group. Each child stands with their back to the white board, marker ready, and then listens while I say the word. Or give each child a personal white board.
