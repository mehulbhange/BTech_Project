Learn- Difference between uppercase and lowercase

Parents should read and apply below points to teach child:

This video will give brief idea about uppercase letters vs lowercase letter and kids are supposed to practice both the letters.

There is not much difference between them but kid is supposed to understand the difference between them so in future there will not be problem while reading sentences and making word.

The alphabet is the building block of literacy and so children must learn to recognize and name the letters, both in and out of order, and the sounds associated with each letter.
