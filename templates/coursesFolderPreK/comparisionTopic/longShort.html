Topic - Long and Short

Parent should explain below points to kids:

Parent should teach kids simply giving following examples:

Comparing Lengths

The parent should select two objects at a time. The two objects were then placed side by side so the children could determine which was longer and which was shorter.The parents helped the children understand that when they compare the items to see which is longer and which is shorter, they needed to first make sure the two items were lined up at the bottom…

Measurement with Towers

when the children played with blocks, that they carried over their learning as they also measured their blocks before building their tall towers. They are starting to understand that if the tower is going to stand, the blocks need to be equal on both sides or it will fall over…

Learning through Play

Isn’t it exciting to see how concepts can be explored and reinforced through their play? so teach your child throgh their play.
