Topic - Same or Different: Part-1

Parent should follow below points while teaching same or different:

Parent should teach kids simply giving following examples:

Pencil Pairs

In the game, ‘Pencil Pairs’, each learner is given a pencil. Next, they walk around the room to find the pencil that matches theirs. To extend the activity, they find a pencil that has only one thing that is the same.

Math Notebook

A Math Notebook is a great way to reduce the amount of papers used in your classroom. It also allows learners to review these concepts throughout the year.Learners CREATE their own pencils that are the same and different with this interactive math notebook page. Simply color and glue to the math notebook.

Find the Difference Math Center

One way for learners to practice finding the difference is with this simple, yet engaging center. Have learners place a manipulative on the picture that is different…super simple! Your learners can complete a printable activity as well!
